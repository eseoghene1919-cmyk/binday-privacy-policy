BinDay UK — Privacy Policy
Last updated: 18th January 2026

BinDay UK (“the App”) is designed to help users check bin collection schedules and set reminders based on their postcode. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect
The App collects the following information:

Postcode (User Input)
You enter your postcode to retrieve your local bin collection schedule.

This data is processed temporarily and is not stored on our servers.

App Activity
Basic, non‑identifying app interaction data may be collected (e.g., which screens are used) to improve functionality.

Approximate Location (Derived from Postcode)
The postcode you enter may be used to determine your local council area.

No GPS or precise location is collected.

2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information you provide to:

Retrieve bin collection schedules for your area

Display relevant information inside the App

Improve App performance and user experience

We do not:

Sell your data

Share your data with third parties

Track you across apps or websites

3. Data Storage and Security
Postcodes are processed ephemerally and are not permanently stored.

All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted in transit.

We do not maintain user accounts or long‑term personal data.

4. Children’s Privacy
The App is intended for general audiences and is not directed at children under 18.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Third‑Party Services
The App does not use third‑party advertising SDKs.
If analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics) are added in the future, this policy will be updated.

6. Your Rights
Since we do not store personal data, there is no persistent information to access, correct, or delete.
If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is removed.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
The latest version will always be available at this URL.
